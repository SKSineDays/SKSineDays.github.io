Information collected

SineDay collects account information, profile/birthdate information, timezone/settings information, planner notes, technical/device data needed to operate the service, and payment-related data processed through Stripe.

SineDay uses this information to authenticate users, provide the dashboard and premium features, generate personalized SineDay outputs, process subscriptions, support customers, and improve service reliability.

Payments are processed by Stripe.
