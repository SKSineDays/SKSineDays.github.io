Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 21, 2026

SineDay is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including people with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures we support

  • Semantic HTML structure and controls
  • Keyboard navigation and visible focus indicators
  • Text alternatives for meaningful imagery
  • Accessible announcements for important UI updates

Known limitations

Some areas of the experience may not yet be fully optimized for every assistive technology. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, we want to hear from you.

Contact

Email: support@sineday.app

Please include the page you were on, what you were trying to do, and your device/browser.
