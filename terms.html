Overview

SineDay is a digital web application that provides personalized wave-based calendar views, printable calendar and planner tools, and cloud-based notes based on a user's birthdate and the SineDay 18-day rhythm system.

Users are responsible for maintaining access to their login credentials and connected email account.

Subscription access

SineDay Premium is a recurring subscription that unlocks premium digital features, including printable premium calendar/planner tools and premium dashboard functionality.

Subscriptions renew automatically until canceled.

Billing and renewal

Billing is processed securely through Stripe.

By purchasing a subscription, the customer authorizes recurring charges according to the selected billing cadence shown at checkout.

Customers may cancel at any time through the SineDay billing portal or account tools.

No prorated refunds are provided for partial billing periods.
